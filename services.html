Process

How I Work

01

Discovery

I begin by understanding your business, goals, target audience, and project requirements. This phase involves research, discussions, and defining the project scope.

02

Strategy & Planning

Based on the discovery phase, I develop a comprehensive strategy and plan that outlines the approach, timeline, and deliverables for your project.

03

Design

I create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes to visualize the final product. This iterative process ensures that the design meets your expectations before development begins.

04

Development

I transform the approved designs into fully functional websites or applications using clean, efficient code and following best practices for performance and accessibility.

05

Testing & Launch

Before launch, I conduct thorough testing to ensure everything works flawlessly across different devices and browsers. Once approved, the project is deployed to a live environment.

06

Support & Maintenance

I provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure your website or application continues to perform optimally and remains up-to-date with the latest technologies.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the project's complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 8-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payment plans to distribute costs throughout the development process.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. These can be tailored to your specific needs and can include regular updates, security monitoring, and content changes.

Can you help with existing websites?

Absolutely! I offer redesign and optimization services for existing websites. This can range from minor updates to complete overhauls, depending on your needs and goals.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business, target audience, goals for the website, and any design preferences. I provide a detailed questionnaire to guide this process and ensure I have all the necessary information.

Do you provide hosting services?

While I don't provide hosting directly, I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs and help set up your website on your chosen hosting platform. I can also manage the hosting relationship on your behalf if desired.
