Featured Case Study

An in-depth look at one of my recent projects

Cartloop SMS Marketing Platform

2024 Web Design, UI/UX Client: Cartloop

Cartloop helps e-commerce brands recover lost sales and build stronger customer relationships through personalized SMS marketing. My task was to refresh their digital presence and improve the platform’s interface for merchants and customers alike.

The Challenge

The existing site communicated value but lacked clarity and visual flow. Important features such as integrations, analytics, and SMS automation needed a more intuitive presentation for new users.

The Solution

I redesigned the platform experience and marketing site with a focus on:

  • Clear value messaging and benefit-driven visuals
  • Streamlined navigation highlighting product features
  • Improved onboarding flow for merchants
  • Responsive layouts optimized for mobile-first users

The Results

+50% Improved Feature Engagement
+30% Faster Merchant Onboarding
+25% Increase in Demo Sign-ups
